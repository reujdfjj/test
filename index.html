What does a resume consist of?
A resume typically includes the following sections:
  • Contact Information: Your name, phone number, email address, and optionally your LinkedIn profile or portfolio link.
  • Professional Summary: A brief overview highlighting your skills, qualifications, and career objectives.
  • Work Experience: A detailed list of your previous jobs, including your job titles, companies, dates of employment, and key achievements.
  • Skills: A section showcasing both technical and soft skills relevant to the job.
  • Education: Information about your degrees, institutions attended, and graduation dates.
  • Certifications and Awards: Any certifications, licenses, or awards that enhance your candidacy.
  • Additional Sections (optional): Depending on your experience, this could include volunteer work, projects, publications, or affiliations relevant to the role.
